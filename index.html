Frequently Asked Questions

We offer early childhood schooling for children ages birth to 6 years old. We offer infant, toddler, and preschool. We do not bus children to and from elementary school.

We are open from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM Monday through Friday. We are open all year round and are closed in observance of major holidays. We are open in the summers, are not closed in the summer like for instance elementary schools.
